Yes. You can pay your membership fees in cash at our location.

If you are a Pay Monthly member, you can freeze and unfreeze your membership at any time in the Member Area online. There is a cost of €5 per month for Pay Monthly members for this and you can freeze for an indefinite period of time. Please make sure to freeze a minimum of 1 day in advance of your next payment when going away. The freeze will last for 30 days at a time and will automatically roll over each month until you unfreeze. When you unfreeze, the next membership instalment is taken immediately as you are always paying in advance for your membership usage. If you are requesting a freeze for genuine medical reasons, injury, pregnancy, etc. A doctor’s cert or letter will be required for this. Please email join@goldstonefitness.ie giving your name, address & reason for seeking to freeze and attach a doctor/physio cert/letter to this email (image jpeg, pdf or word doc. only). So, if you have the cert/letter but don’t have access to a computer, just take a picture of it with your phone and email it.
