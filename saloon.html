Spa:

A health club spa brings fitness and spa into one space. Facilities will often include a large gym or fitness space, regular classes as well as the usual wet and thermal spa facilities. Most will offer a range of memberships alongside their spa days or breaks.
Spa treatments often include a combination of: deep cleansing,
massages and moisturisation.
Body treatments,
facials and scrubs not only remove impurities, but they also help soften the skin.

Benefits:

On top of how incredibly relaxing massage can be, it can also benefit our body in that it can improve blood flow and circulation. This helps our body to fend off illness. This is because your lungs, your heart, and your muscles are able to function properly.
